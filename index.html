Welcome to Moving '95
Your all-in-one moving command centre — built to keep every box, bin, route, and task in one place while you move.
💡 Tap the logo in the top-left at any time to cycle colour modes — Light, Dark… and something extra. 👀
📦
Inventory
Log every item with room, type, weight, photo notes and a bin/box number. Each item gets its own auto-numbered ID so nothing gets lost.
💡 Create Bins & Boxes first — they get smart room-prefix IDs like KT-001 (Kitchen) or ST-003 (Studio). Qty lets you create a whole set at once.
🗺️
Route Planner
Plan your full driving route with multiple stops, get distance and time estimates, and find gas stations along the way.
💡 Use Share My Route to text or email your route + live GPS location to a contact — great for keeping people updated on moving day.
💰✅
Budget & Tasks
Track every moving expense in the Budget tab and stay on top of your to-do list with the Tasks checklist — mark items done as you go.
💡 Tasks sync with your move schedule. Set dates on calendar events so nothing sneaks up on you.
🎮🎧
Entertainment
Take a break with Solitaire, Snake, Sudoku, Trivia and more. When you need a soundtrack, flip to Radio.
📻 The radio stations are hand-picked — every genre, every mood, curated just for this app. Dial through and find your vibe.
🕹️
One Last Thing…
You already know about colour modes. But did you notice that one of them unlocks extra features in some sections? Explore and find out.
✨ Tap the logo a few times and see what happens. Some things only come out when the lights go down.
